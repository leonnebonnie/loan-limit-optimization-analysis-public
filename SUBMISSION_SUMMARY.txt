   • Created 6 engineered features (credit score, interactions, ratios)

   • Credit Scoring: Multi-factor weighted proxy

   • 3-state quarterly transitions
   • Empirically-derived transition matrix

   • Greedy allocation with multi-constraint satisfaction
   • Constraints: Eligibility, max increases, risk limits
   • Objective: Maximize NPV with 19% discount rate

5. MONTE CARLO SIMULATION
   • 100,000 stochastic scenarios
   • 4-quarter lifecycle modeling
   • Dynamic risk transitions

6. NPV ANALYSIS
   • Quarterly discounting at 19% annual rate
   • Sensitivity analysis across discount rates
   • Portfolio-level aggregation
